## Version 1.2.3
{: #tke-cli-123}

Version 1.2.3 of the TKE CLI was released on 30 June 2021.

Added the `ibmcloud tke failover-enable` command to enable or add failover crypto units after provisioning a {{site.data.keyword.hscrypto}} instance.
